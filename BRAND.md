# Ledgable — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Essence
**Simple. Smart. Australian.**
Ledgable is the accounting software built for people who run businesses, not people who study accounting.

## Brand Voice
- Direct, never corporate
- Confident without being arrogant
- Warm — like a smart friend who happens to be a CPA
- Australian in attitude (no fluff, gets things done)

---

## Colour System

### Primary — Deep Teal
- **Primary:** `#0D9488` (teal-600)
- **Dark:** `#0F766E` (teal-700) — hover/active states
- **Darker:** `#134E4A` (teal-900) — headings, authority
- **Light:** `#CCFBF1` (teal-100) — backgrounds, tags
- **Pale:** `#F0FDFA` (teal-50) — page backgrounds

### Accent — Amber
- **Accent:** `#F59E0B` (amber-500) — CTAs, highlights, "money moments"
- **Accent Dark:** `#D97706` (amber-600) — hover
- **Accent Light:** `#FEF3C7` (amber-100) — badges, tags

### Neutrals

## Typography

### Headings — Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Font:** Plus Jakarta Sans (Google Fonts)
- **Weights:** 700 (bold), 800 (extrabold)
- **H1:** 56px / line-height 1.1 / tracking -0.02em
- **H2:** 40px / line-height 1.2 / tracking -0.01em
- **H3:** 28px / line-height 1.3
- **H4:** 20px / line-height 1.4

### Body — Inter
- **Font:** Inter (system stack fallback)
- **Weights:** 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold)
- **Body:** 16px / line-height 1.6
- **Small:** 14px / line-height 1.5
- **Tiny:** 12px / line-height 1.5

### Monospace — JetBrains Mono
- **Use for:** Numbers, amounts, codes, data
- **Size:** Match surrounding context

---

## Logo Mark
- **Symbol:** A stylised ledger "L" that doubles as a rising chart line
- **Style:** Geometric, minimal, confident
- **Primary:** White on teal-600 background OR teal-600 on white
- **Don't use:** On busy backgrounds, never stretch or distort

---

## Component Design Language

### Cards
- White background, `shadow-sm`, `rounded-xl` (12px)
- Border: 1px `border-slate-200`
- Padding: 24px

### Buttons
- **Primary:** `bg-teal-600 text-white hover:bg-teal-700 rounded-lg px-4 py-2.5 font-semibold`
- **Secondary:** `border border-teal-600 text-teal-600 hover:bg-teal-50 rounded-lg`
- **Danger:** `bg-red-500 text-white hover:bg-red-600`
- **Ghost:** `text-slate-600 hover:bg-slate-100`

### Nav / Sidebar
- Background: teal-900 (#134E4A)
- Active items: teal-600 pill
- Text: white/70% for inactive, white for active

### Dashboard Widgets
- Metric numbers: Plus Jakarta Sans, 700, teal-600 or slate-900
- Chart colors: teal-500 (income), red-400 (expense), amber-400 (GST)
- Stat cards: White, rounded-xl, subtle shadow

---

## App Icon
- Deep teal square with rounded corners (like iOS)
- White "L" / ledger mark
- Clean, readable at 20px

---

## What Ledgable Is NOT
- Not a bank (don't use piggy banks, vaults, safes)
- Not boring (no clip art, no stock photos of people pointing at laptops)
- Not complicated (no dashboards with 47 widgets)
- Not blue (Xero owns blue — we own teal)
